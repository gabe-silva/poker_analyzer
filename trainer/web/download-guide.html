PokerNow Hand Log Download Guide

How to export PokerNow hand history JSON files for upload into PokerNow Trainer.

Plans Analyzer Setup Trainer Download Guide Account

Quick Summary

  1. Open LOG / LEDGER from your PokerNow table.
  2. In Session Log, click REPLAYER.
  3. In Replayer, click the "clicking here" link to download.
  4. Go back to Analyzer and upload the downloaded file.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

Use these screenshots in order after your game finishes.

Step 1: Open LOG / LEDGER on the table

Drop file: tutorial-step-1-log-ledger.png

Click LOG / LEDGER on the bottom-left corner of the table.

Step 2: Click REPLAYER in Session Log

Drop file: tutorial-step-2-replayer-button.png

From Session Log, click the REPLAYER button near the bottom-right.

Step 3: Download from Replayer link

Drop file: tutorial-step-3-clicking-here-link.png

Select a hand and use the highlighted "clicking here" link to download the log file.

After Download

  1. Open Analyzer.
  2. Click Choose Files and select your downloaded JSON file(s).
  3. Click Upload Hand Files.
  4. Select player name(s), then run analysis.
