Access

Pick a tier below. The steps shown will update based on your selection.

Free Flow

  1. Enter your email.
  2. Click Start Free to send a login code.
  3. Paste code and click Verify & Sign In.

Pro Flow

  1. Enter your email.
  2. New users: click Continue With New Pro Account to open Stripe checkout.
  3. Returning users: skip checkout, click Email Login Code, then Verify & Sign In.
  4. Use Manage Billing to update or cancel subscriptions.

Elite Flow

  1. Enter your email.
  2. New users: click Continue With New Elite Account to open Stripe checkout.
  3. Returning users: skip checkout, click Email Login Code, then Verify & Sign In.
  4. Use Manage Billing to update or cancel subscriptions.
